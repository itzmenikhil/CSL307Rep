Shield The Fire (_Game_)

Introduction

In this game shots are fired from the tank and the player needs to protect the camps from getting destroyed using a shield .
Our Prototyped version of the game is demonstrated using a simple GIF .

Rules for our game

1) For each shot saved by the player he is awarded 10 points .
2) If three camps gets destroyed the player looses .
3) The Player can use let arrow and right arrow to move to cover and protect the camps .

The proposed prototype of our game

Overview of our game

The Player needs to protect his camps from shots fired at them using a shield .
If the Player Looses to protect his camps from the shots fired at them and if th3 of his camps get destroyed then he looses .
