Shield The Fire (_Game_)

Introduction

In this game shots are fired and the player needs to protect the stars from getting destroyed using a shield .
We have created a simple GIF to demonstrate the prototyped gameplay .

Prototype of our game

Overview of our game

The Player needs to protect his stars from shots fired at them using a shield .
If the Player Looses to protect his stars from the shots fired at them and if all the stars are destroyed then he looses .
If he can protect his stars till the bullets complete ,then he wins the game !!
